Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and plans

Are there any setup or hidden fees?

No, there are no setup fees or hidden charges with SecurePay. You only pay the monthly subscription fee and the transaction fees listed in your plan. We believe in complete transparency with our pricing.

Can I change plans as my business grows?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your account dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. We've made it easy to scale your payment solution as your business evolves.

How do refunds work with your pricing?

For refunded transactions, we refund the percentage fee, but the fixed fee ($0.30) is non-refundable. This helps cover the processing costs we incur even when a transaction is refunded.

Do you offer any discounts for nonprofits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for registered nonprofit organizations. Our nonprofit rates start at 2.2% + $0.30 per transaction. Please contact our sales team and provide your nonprofit documentation to apply for these rates.

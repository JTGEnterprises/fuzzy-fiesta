Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How secure is your payment gateway?

SecurePay uses bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL) and is fully PCI DSS Level 1 compliant, which is the highest level of security certification in the payment industry. We also implement tokenization, fraud monitoring, and regular security audits to protect all transactions.

How quickly can I implement your payment solution?

Most businesses can start accepting payments within 24-48 hours of signing up. Our integration process is streamlined with clear documentation, pre-built libraries for popular platforms, and dedicated support to help you get started quickly.

What payment methods do you support?

SecurePay supports all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), ACH transfers, and local payment methods in over 30 countries. We continually add new payment methods to help you reach more customers.

What are your transaction fees?

Our standard pricing is 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction for online payments. We offer volume-based discounts for businesses processing over $50,000 monthly. Please contact our sales team for a custom quote based on your specific needs and transaction volume.
